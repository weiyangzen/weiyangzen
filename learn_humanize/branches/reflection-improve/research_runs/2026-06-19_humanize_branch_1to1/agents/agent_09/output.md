Malformed RLCR state fails closed (`hooks/loop-bash-validator.sh:187-190`). `state.md`, `finalize-state.md`, and `methodology-analysis-state.md` are loop-owned and protected as both write destinations and `mv`/`cp` sources (`hooks/loop-bash-validator.sh:245-271`, `hooks/loop-bash-validator.sh:284-430`). - dependencies_and_callers: Registered as the Bash PreToolUse hook in `hooks/hooks.json:42-48`.
